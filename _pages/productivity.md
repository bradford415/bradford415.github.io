A lot of personal sites I've taken inspiration from have a section on their productivity tips so I decided to include one as well. In general, many producitvity techniques are similar, however, everyone is different and has some uniqueness to themselves which makes them productive. The tips below are a mix of well-known techniques and a few that are just things I’ve found helpful myself.


1. If a task takes you 5 minutes or less, finish it immediately
* If the short task is finished immediately when it pops up there's no need to worry about it any longer, you won't have to write it down, and you can go back to mainly thinking about your primary task
2. Take advantage of your flow state
* It's very easy to get out of your flow state with unrelated things
* Tell yourself you're going to complete some milestone (like finishing a function, or reading a section in a research paper), then you'll allow yourself to grab that snack or catch up with a coworker
